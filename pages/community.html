Batman Unburied Spotify Podcast Sets Release Date

Spotify has announced the release date for their exclusive podcast Batman Unburied, which stars Black Panther star Winston Duke as Bruce Wayne. The original podcast project was created and executive produced by The Dark Knight writer David S. Goyer. DC previously announced the Spotify series as part of a multi-year, first-look licensing deal for podcasts based on popular DC characters.
